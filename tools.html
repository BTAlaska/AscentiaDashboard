Bridge Posture

This page tracks the editor-external tools Ascentia expects to bridge: Landscry, ARPG Asset Factory, Game World Asset Factory, Skin Forge / InterfaceArtForge, Ultra Dynamic Sky, and Fab / Marketplace intake. The product decision is not whether these names are useful. The decision is whether Ascentia has an adapter, proof path, and promotion gate around their output.

BuyUse the provider for the hard commodity problem. BridgeConvert provider output into Ascentia profiles and staging roots. BuildOwn the Ascentia-specific contract, validator, and editor surface. ValidatePublish evidence before production promotion or cooked-build claims.

Tool Bridges

5 bridges

Landscry

World

Landscry output is source material, not final game structure. It enters staging, then Ascentia classifies terrain, roads, cliffs, foliage, POIs, and props before any production promotion.

Ascentia bridgeEUW_AscentiaWorldDirector, EUW_AscentiaLandscryImportAudit, EUW_AscentiaPCGProvenanceGate, PDA_WorldZone, PDA_WorldGenerationProfile.
Evidence postureStaging import report, Data Layer/HLOD coverage, PCG seed/provenance, nav-affecting actor count, scale/bounds checks, zone signoff, and latest pass/fail dashboard card.
Promotion gateNo generated terrain, POI, enemy, loot, or gameplay actor becomes production structure until provenance, stable world identity, gameplay replacement, and human verdict pass.
Evidence rootSaved/AscentiaEvidence/P17/LandscryImport/{RunId}/report.json

Asset Factory

Art

Asset Factory generates armor and weapon drafts. Ascentia owns fit, socket policy, collision, LOD/Nanite decisions, materials, provenance, and the Designer accept/reject verdict.

Ascentia bridgeEUW_AscentiaModularFitLab, EUW_AscentiaWeaponMountLab, EUW_AscentiaLODAndCollisionDoctor, and EUW_AscentiaPackIngestor.
Evidence postureFit/clipping screenshot sheet, socket-profile draft, equip-preview variants, collision and LOD report, material policy check, source/license notes, and provenance report.
Promotion gateGenerated variants can draft socket profiles, but production item rows cannot reference them until Designer verdict and source/provenance evidence exist.
Evidence rootSaved/AscentiaEvidence/Tools/WeaponMountLab/{RunId}/report.json

Game World Asset Factory

World

The standalone factory plans thematic terrain/PCG meshes, props, and reviewed decorative attachments for Nanite, PCG, Worldheart mounting, and manual dressing. Buildings and structural meshes belong to Worldheart. Paid work is stopped; the browser exposes a locked v3 catalog of 25 archetypes and 1,125 plans. Provider or Blender completion does not prove a production mesh.

Ascentia bridgeFuture P17/P23 world-asset quarantine intake, EUW_AscentiaLODAndCollisionDoctor, PCG provenance tags, and world-zone review.
Evidence posturePrompt/catalog hash, Meshy responsibility, Images 2 request, Meshy task IDs and downloads, deterministic Blender scale/pivot/topology/material QA, four inspection views, Unreal recipe, and human Nanite/PCG/Worldheart-mount verdict. The local browser separates complete Images 2 references from live 3D models, supports full-screen and unlit/neutral review, and links each landed card to its GLB and canonical ImportQueue package path. Fifty completed packages remain current-scope candidates; 29 structural packages are historical quarantine only. Explicit Blender warnings remain and Unreal proof is absent.
Promotion gateNo mesh bypasses ImportQueue/WorldAssets. The old v2 approval is invalid; any future work requires a new exact v3 user decision, hash, ID list, and cap. Structural output can never be promoted as Meshy-owned content. Unreal quarantine, collision, Nanite, PCG placement, Worldheart mounting, and human review remain separate gates.
Evidence rootD:/Ascentia/repos/game-world-asset-factory/{generated,ImportQueue/WorldAssets,PromptReviews}

Skin Forge / InterfaceArtForge

UI

Generated UI skins are visual direction, not UI authority. Ascentia keeps WidgetBlueprint bindings, CommonUI behavior, input glyphs, localization, navigation, and accessibility readable.

Ascentia bridgeEUW_AscentiaSkinForgeBridge, UMGToolSet operations, CommonUI style assets, EUW_AscentiaInputGlyphBinder, and text-scale/contrast checks.
Evidence postureWBP skin application report, missing widget binding warnings, style variance screenshots, controller/mouse navigation proof, pseudo-localization screenshots, and contrast report.
Promotion gateNo generated skin ships unless bindings, navigation, glyphs, localization, text scale, and contrast survive the bridge without breaking native UI state.
Evidence rootSaved/AscentiaEvidence/UX and Saved/AscentiaEvidence/Accessibility

Ultra Dynamic Sky

Atmosphere

UDS is an art-direction provider behind Ascentia atmosphere profiles. Weather, time, mood, materials, audio, VFX, saves, performance, and readability must remain visible to Ascentia tools.

Ascentia bridgeEUW_AscentiaAtmosphereDirector, UAscentiaUDSAtmosphereAdapter, IAscentiaAtmosphereProvider, PDA_LightingMoodProfile, PDA_WeatherProfile, and PDA_TimeOfDayProfile.
Evidence postureWeather/time screenshot grid, lighting readability report, material/weather response check, audio/VFX budget rows, performance buckets, save/load transition proof, and gameplay opt-in rows.
Promotion gateRaw UDS actor edits are prototype-only. Cooked builds should block profile bypasses when atmosphere touches gameplay, saves, readability, performance, or accessibility.
Evidence rootSaved/AscentiaEvidence/P17/{Atmosphere,WeatherTime,LightingReadability,AtmospherePerformance}

Fab / Marketplace Intake

Intake

Fab and Marketplace intake is governance, not drag-and-drop content. Ascentia records source, license, plugin risk, staging roots, approval state, attribution needs, and upgrade regressions.

Ascentia bridgeEUW_AscentiaExternalContentRegistry, EUW_AscentiaLicenseComplianceBoard, EUW_AscentiaExternalAssetQuarantine, EUW_AscentiaPluginDescriptorDoctor, EUW_AscentiaExternalUpgradePlanner, and EUW_AscentiaExternalContentPromotionGate.
Evidence postureSource rows, purchase/version dates, license policy, plugin descriptor risk, quarantine findings, promotion report, attribution/NoAI checks, and linked regression scenarios.
Promotion gateEvery promoted asset needs a source record. Unknown downloads, missing license rows, standalone redistribution risk, dangerous references, or unresolved plugin risk block promotion.
Evidence rootSaved/AscentiaEvidence/P23/{ExternalContent,LicenseCompliance,PluginDescriptors}
